Reflection

My social media work has allowed me to develop a strong understanding of creating content tailored for digital audiences while balancing creativity with clear communication. Through projects such as producing promotional content for an independent clothing startup and creating event-focused reels for the Brighton and Sussex Malayali Society, I explored how visual style, pacing, and editing choices can be adapted to suit different brand identities and audience expectations. These projects strengthened my skills in short-form video editing, visual storytelling, and platform-specific content creation, while also giving me experience in translating a client or organisation’s vision into engaging social media content.
